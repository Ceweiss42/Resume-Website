Go Kart

My biggest real engineering project is a Go Kart! It runs a 1.8kW motor and tops out around 35mph (56 kph). It is almost entirely custom built, being pieced together out of parts you can find out of home depot, with a couple items coming as is, such as the chassis, wheels, stub axle, and brakes.

While it currently runs on battery, I am actively working on adding a 1kW Hydrogen fuel cell to run the kart on hydrogen-electric power. Also in the works is a Arduino based EMS, which should increase efficiency while allowing the kart to run for longer.
